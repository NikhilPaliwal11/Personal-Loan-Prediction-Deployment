Personal Loan Prediction

Web App, Built with Flask and Deployed using Heroku

The project is related to Bank loan modelling and to identify the customer who have a higher probality of purchasing Personal loan.

Mortage Value :
Securities Account :
CD Account:
Online :
Credict Card :


Column Description
Age - Customer's age in completed years [eg. 34]
Income - Annual income of the customer ($000) [eg. 180]
Family - Family size of the customer [eg. 1]
CCAvg - Avg. Spending on credit cards per month ($000) [eg. 5.2]
Education - Education Level. 1: Undergrad; 2: Graduate; 3: Advanced/Professional [eg. Advanced/Professional]
Mortgage - Value of house mortgage if any ($000) [eg. 0]
Securities Account - Customer have a securities account with the bank(1 = yes, 0 = No) [eg. No]
CD Account - customer have a certificate of deposit (CD) account with the bank(1 = yes, 0 = No) [eg. No]
Online - Customer use internet banking facilities(1 = yes, 0 = No) [eg. Yes]
CreditCard - Customer uses a credit card issued by UniversalBank.(1 = yes, 0 = No) [eg. Yes]
